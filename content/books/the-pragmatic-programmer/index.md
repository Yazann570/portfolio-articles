These are my personal notes from reading *The Pragmatic Programmer*.
I am not trying to summarize the whole book. I am writing the ideas I want to remember, connect, and apply in my own work.

## Main lessons

* Take responsibility for your work.
* Fix small problems before they become normal.
* Start change with small visible progress.
* Build good-enough software based on real needs.
* Keep learning continuously.
* Communicate clearly.
