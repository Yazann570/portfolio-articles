## Main lessons

- Take responsibility for your work instead of making excuses.
- Fix small problems early before they become normal in the project.
- Be a catalyst for change by starting with small visible progress.
- Build software that is good enough for real user needs, not perfect forever.
- Keep investing in your knowledge portfolio through continuous learning.
- Communicate clearly with the right message for the right audience.
- Avoid duplication and keep your design flexible.
- Use simple tools, automation, testing, and teamwork to improve the whole project.
